Recognition Awards

Our special trophies are made from recycled materials donated by our contributors - turning waste into symbols of achievement!

Eco Champion Trophy

Made from recycled aluminum and plastic bottles. This beautiful trophy represents the highest level of contribution and environmental commitment.

Criteria: 500+ items donated in a month, active community engagement, and mentoring other contributors.

Sustainability Star Medal

Crafted from recycled copper and glass, this medal celebrates consistent contributions and innovative recycling ideas.

Criteria: 200+ items donated monthly for 3 consecutive months, plus one innovative recycling project.

Green Contributor Badge

Made from recycled cardboard and fabric, this badge recognizes new contributors and encourages continued participation.

Criteria: 50+ items donated in first month as a contributor, showing commitment to the cause.

How to Become a Contributor

Join our community of eco-warriors! Follow these simple steps to start making a positive impact today.

1

Sign Up

Register as a contributor on our platform with your student ID. It's free and takes less than 2 minutes!

2

Collect Materials

Gather recyclable materials from home, school, or community. We accept paper, plastic, electronics, textiles, and more.

3

Sort & Weigh

Sort materials by type and weigh them. Use our mobile app to log your contributions and track your impact.

4

Drop Off

Bring your materials to designated collection points at school or schedule a pickup for larger contributions.
