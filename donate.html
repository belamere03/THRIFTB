Materials We Accept

We transform a wide variety of materials into beautiful, functional products

Plastic Materials

Plastic Bottles & Containers

Water bottles, food containers, shampoo bottles (clean and dry)

Plastic Bags & Wrapping

Shopping bags, packaging materials, bubble wrap

Plastic Toys & Items

Broken toys, storage bins, plastic kitchenware

Not Accepted: Hazardous Plastics

Medical waste, chemical containers, broken electronics casings

Glass Materials

Glass Bottles & Jars

Wine bottles, jam jars, sauce bottles (labels removed)

Glassware & Dishes

Cups, plates, bowls, decorative glass items

Window Glass & Mirrors

Small window panes, mirror pieces, glass sheets

Not Accepted: Broken Glass

Shattered glass, car windshields, tempered glass

Paper Materials

Newspapers & Magazines

Old newspapers, magazines, brochures, flyers

Cardboard & Packaging

Cardboard boxes, egg cartons, paper packaging

Books & Notebooks

Old textbooks, notebooks, office paper

Not Accepted: Contaminated Paper

Greasy pizza boxes, wet paper, wax-coated paper

Metal Materials

Aluminum Cans & Foil

Soda cans, aluminum foil, food trays

Steel & Iron Items

Food cans, small appliances, metal tools

Copper & Brass

Old pipes, decorative items, hardware

Not Accepted: Hazardous Metals

Lead-based items, radioactive materials, large appliances

Fabric Materials

Clothing & Textiles

Old clothes, bed sheets, curtains, towels

Cotton & Natural Fibers

Cotton fabrics, wool items, linen materials

Synthetic Fabrics

Polyester, nylon, synthetic blends

Not Accepted: Contaminated Fabrics

Moldy or heavily stained items, hazmat clothing

Electronics & Other

Small Electronics

Old phones, calculators, small gadgets

Cables & Accessories

USB cables, chargers, headphones

Wood & Natural Materials

Small wood pieces, cork, bamboo items

Not Accepted: Large Electronics

TVs, refrigerators, washing machines, batteries

Donation Guidelines

Clean & Dry

Please ensure all items are clean and completely dry before donation

Remove Labels

Remove stickers, labels, and tape from containers when possible

Sort by Type

Group similar materials together for easier processing

Pack Safely

Use sturdy bags or boxes, especially for fragile items

Our Donation Process

From your donation to finished product - here's how your materials make an impact

1

Collection & Intake

Your donated materials arrive at our collection points where volunteers and students conduct initial sorting and quality assessment.

Quality check performed Materials logged into system Donation receipt issued
2

Sorting & Categorization

Materials are carefully sorted by type, condition, and potential use. Our student teams identify the best transformation opportunities for each item.

Materials categorized by type Condition assessment completed Project assignments made
3

Cleaning & Preparation

Materials undergo thorough cleaning and preparation. Our workshop team removes adhesives, disassembles complex items, and prepares materials for transformation.

Deep cleaning process Material preparation Safety inspection
4

Design & Planning

Student designers create innovative concepts for transforming your materials. Each design considers functionality, aesthetics, and environmental impact.

Creative design concepts Technical feasibility check Resource planning
5

Transformation & Creation

The magic happens here! Students use workshops, maker spaces, and creative techniques to transform your donated materials into beautiful, functional products.

Hands-on manufacturing Quality craftsmanship Student skill development
6

Quality Control & Finishing

Every product undergoes rigorous quality testing and finishing touches. We ensure each item meets our high standards for durability and aesthetics.

Quality assurance testing Final finishing touches Product photography
7

Sale & Distribution

Finished products are listed in our online and physical stores. Each item includes information about its origin and the students who created it.

Product listing & pricing Story documentation Sales tracking
8

Charity Impact

100% of proceeds go directly to our partner charities. You receive updates on how your donation contributed to positive community impact.

Funds transferred to charity Impact reporting Donor recognition
